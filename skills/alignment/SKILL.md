# Alignment

Lock intent before you write code. Make what we will do and won't do explicit, and agree on standards and constraints up front.

## Principles

- **Clear, testable requirements**: Precise and agreed upon before implementation.
- **Explicit scope boundaries**: Define Scope In and Scope Out.
- **Definition of Done**: Establish upfront so everyone agrees when work is complete.
- **Non-negotiable constraints**: Engineering norms and safeguards are hard constraints.

## Practices

- Complete Requirements (R) and Safeguards (S) of the REASONS Canvas first.
- Use Given/When/Then criteria with concrete examples.
- Mark gaps `[NEEDS CLARIFICATION]` rather than assuming.
- Re-read the structured prompt before generating code to confirm alignment.

## Checklist

- Requirements precise enough to generate tests?
- Scope boundaries (in and out) explicit?
- All acceptance criteria testable?
- Norms and safeguards defined and agreed?
